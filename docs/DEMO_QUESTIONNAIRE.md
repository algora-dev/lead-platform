# Demo discovery questionnaire

1. What does your business sell?
2. Who is your ideal customer?
3. Which countries or regions do you target?
4. What event suggests that a prospect may need your service?
5. Where do you currently find prospects?
6. Do you already have data you can upload?
7. Which public sources contain useful signals?
8. What information must every result contain?
9. Which information is useful but optional?
10. What are the five strongest positive buying signals?
11. Which signal should carry the most weight?
12. Does repeat activity from the same company increase value?
13. What company sizes are most valuable?
14. What spend, salary, contract value or budget signal matters?
15. What makes a result urgent?
16. Which contact methods are required or preferred?
17. How should high, medium and low priority results be handled?
18. Which columns must appear in the dashboard?
19. What name, logo and colours should the demo use?
20. What action should a user take after finding a strong result?
