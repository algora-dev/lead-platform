# Product brief

## The product
A white-label data intelligence platform that collects or accepts records, extracts signals, scores and segments them, and presents actionable opportunities in a branded dashboard.

Core flow:

`Collect or upload → normalise → extract → aggregate by company → score → enrich → review → outreach/export`

## First tenant
The internal AI consultancy tenant searches Brave in the UK and New Zealand for job adverts containing operational work such as CRM updates, data entry, scheduling, customer enquiries, reporting, document creation, ordering and administration.

The job advert is not the final lead. It is evidence that the employer:
- has a problem it wants solved;
- is prepared to spend money;
- may have adjacent workflow, AI and automation opportunities.

## Scoring philosophy
All scoring is additive. Positive evidence includes:
- one or more active adverts;
- multiple adverts from the same company;
- repeated task categories across adverts;
- disclosed salary investment;
- public contact details;
- estimated company size, with 10–150 employees currently weighted as the sweet spot;
- breadth of operational task evidence.

A score is a prioritisation aid, not a rejection gate.

## Commercial template
A future prospect should answer a short questionnaire. A demo should then be created mainly by changing tenant configuration:
- branding;
- sources and queries;
- fields and extraction targets;
- scoring rules;
- terminology;
- feature flags.

The customer should be able to open a branded dashboard, click Scan or upload data, and immediately see ranked opportunities and transparent score reasons.
